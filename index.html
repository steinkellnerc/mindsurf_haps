The Problem

Germany has a
connectivity crisis.

Despite being Europe's largest economy, Germany consistently ranks among the worst in the EU for mobile and broadband coverage. Rural communities, train corridors, and even suburban areas are routinely underserved or completely offline.

Ground infrastructure is slow and expensive to build. Fibre rollout lags years behind schedule. Existing solutions treat connectivity as an afterthought. The gaps aren't being closed fast enough.

HAPS changes the geometry entirely — one stratospheric platform can cover an area the size of a German federal state. That's not an incremental improvement. That's a structural fix.

The Architecture

Three layers.
One seamless network.

Myndsurf Air sits at the distribution layer — connecting end users to the emerging stratospheric infrastructure through a single, clean product experience.

01 🛸

HAPS Infrastructure Partners

Solar-powered autonomous gliders at 20km altitude — operated by our infrastructure partners including Airbus Zephyr, HAPSMobile, and Aalto. Each platform covers up to 200km radius, providing capacity comparable to a terrestrial cell network. We distribute their signal; they build and fly the platforms.

02 📡

Myndsurf Air Distribution Layer

We are the commercial and consumer interface between HAPS infrastructure and end users. Think of us as the brand and billing layer — packaging stratospheric connectivity into simple plans, managing e-SIM provisioning, and building the customer relationships that infrastructure companies don't focus on.

03 📱

Direct to Device

Your existing 5G smartphone, a simple e-SIM download, and you're connected — no specialist hardware required for mobile coverage. For homes and offices, the Myndsurf Air Hub provides a high-bandwidth fixed connection, independent of any ground cabling or terrestrial ISP.
