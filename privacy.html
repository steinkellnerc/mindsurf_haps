Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Myndsurf Air Inc.

This policy applies to data collected via air.myndsurf.com — our waitlist, coverage registration, and contact forms. We are in pre-launch phase and handle only the data strictly necessary to operate the waitlist.

1. Who we are

Myndsurf Air Inc. is the data controller for all personal data collected via this website. For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at hello@myndsurf.com.

2. What data we collect

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Postcode / postal code — optionally provided, used solely to map coverage demand.
  • IP address and browser metadata — collected automatically by our infrastructure for security and analytics.

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

  • Email / postcode: Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) — you provide this voluntarily to receive launch notifications.
  • Technical logs: Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — to maintain security and service reliability.

4. How we use it

We use your email to notify you when Myndsurf Air launches in your area and to share relevant product updates. We will not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data storage

Data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore (EU data centres). Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@myndsurf.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Firebase may set technical cookies required for session management.

8. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy as our product evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via email to waitlist members.

9. Supervisory authority

If you are based in Germany, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent German data protection supervisory authority. The federal authority is the BfDI (Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit): bfdi.bund.de.
